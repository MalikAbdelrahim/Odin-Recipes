GrilledCheese

Description:

GrilledCheese is a childhood classic and is one of the easiest foods to make. With a tasty cheese in between 2 slices of toast and a little butter you can't go wrong.

Ingredients:

  • Toast
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Put a pan onto the stove on high heat
  2. Coat the pan with butter
  3. Once hot lay one slice of toast on the pan with cheese in the middle and another slice of toast on top to make a sandwich
  4. Once the toast on the bottom has become golden and the cheese melts a bit, flip over the sandwich to the unheated side
  5. Wait till the toast is golden and your done
