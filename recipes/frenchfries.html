French Fries

Description:

French Fries are another childhood classic and provide you with an amazing hot,salty, and crispy snack or side to a meal.

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Ketchup(optional)

Steps:

  1. Slice the potatoes 1/2 inch thick.
  2. Soak them in cold water for at least a hour or overnight
  3. Rinse them twice with cold water and pat them dry
  4. Heat oil to 300 degrees.
  5. Fry in about 6 batches for 5-6 minutes
  6. Increase heat to 400 degree. Fry in batch until golden brown(about 5 mins)
  7. Place them on paper towels and sprinkle with salt. Use ketchup as a great dipping sauce.
