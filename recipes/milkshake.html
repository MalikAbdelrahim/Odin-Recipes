MilkShake

Description:

A milkshake is wonderful concoction of milk, ice cream, whipping cream, and sprinkles. It has a very sweet and sugary taste but with a few changes you can have different flavors. The most popular of milkshakes are vanilla but with different ice cream flavors and adding other elements you can make almost any flavor of your choice!

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Ice Cream (Chocolate if preferred)
  • Whipping Cream
  • Sprinkles
